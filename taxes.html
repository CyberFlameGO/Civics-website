Saffron Tax Information And Assistance Program

In Saffron City we know that taxes can be hard to pay or manage.

That's why we are proud to say our Tax Assistance Program has been approved and is in the works for our citizens!

We have a range of options to help our citizens with there taxes.

Tax Assistance Program

Our tax Assistance Program can help family's Pay there taxes, manage there taxes, and plan payments for there taxes.

If you qualify for this program you must visit your local town-hall or goverment building.

You will fill out forms requesting to get into the program. With in 1-2 weeks you will get a call and a letter stating weither you are in or not.

When accepted you will be given a time and location to talk to a profeshinal. They will construct a plan with you to help pay off or manage taxes.

This does not work with federal taxes!!! This Program only works with state taxes.

Tax Assistance Program Requirements

  • You must have a job
  • You must have no pending felines
  • You must have a address in Saffron City
  • You must have a credit score over 460
  • You must have a cosigner who will share liability
